## About Alter

**Alter** is a user-centric mobile application reshaping sustainable waste management. Users effortlessly earn "Alter Coins" by recycling products through the app, offering a seamless pathway to redeem tokens for purchasing recycled goods or making charitable donations. Alter simplifies recycling processes, fostering user engagement in eco-friendly practices and promoting environmental responsibility. The innovative use of Alter Coins contributes to the development of a circular economy, establishing a sustainable and rewarding approach to waste disposal.
